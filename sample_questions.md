# Sample Questions for WalletGenie

## General
- "What is my wallet summary?"
- "How much did I spend on Food last month?"
- "What are my top spending categories?"

## Financial Health Score (Insight Agent)
- "What is my financial health score?"
- "Why is my health score low?"
- "How can I improve my financial health?"

## Savings & Goals (Savings Strategist)
- "I want to save $5000 in 6 months. What should I change?"
- "Create a savings goal for a new car."
- "Simulate a savings plan for a vacation."

## Transaction Analysis (Normaliser/Insight)
- "Show me my recent transactions."
- "Did I overspend on weekends?"
- "Do I have any recurring subscriptions?"
